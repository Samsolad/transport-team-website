The Problem

Sound familiar?

If you organise transport for your church, school, or community programme, you already know these problems by heart.

📱

The WhatsApp chaos

Members say they are coming, then do not show. The driver waits. Fuel is wasted. You spend your morning on the phone chasing people.

❓

No one knows where the bus is

Families stand outside not knowing if the bus is 2 minutes away or 20. They call the driver. The driver is trying to concentrate on the road.

📋

The spreadsheet nightmare

Someone has a list. It is out of date. Nobody told the driver that three people are not coming today. Three unnecessary stops later, everyone is late.

😰

One person carries everything

The transport coordinator knows everyone's address, who needs what support, and which bus goes where. If they are not available, the whole thing falls apart.

How it works

Up and running in three steps.

No IT department needed. No hardware to install. If you can use a smartphone, you can run Transport Team.

01
🏢

Set up your organisation

Create your workspace in minutes. Add your buses, register your drivers, and set your transport programmes. You get a unique QR code to share with your members.

02
📍

Members register once

Families and passengers scan your QR code, enter your invite code, and register their pickup address. That is the only time they ever fill in a form.

03
🔔

Daily — just one tap

Each morning, members toggle whether they are going today. Drivers see exactly who to collect. Passengers get notified when the driver is 10, 5, and 2 minutes away.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built specifically for the way community transport actually works — not how enterprise software thinks it works.

🔒

Complete organisation isolation

Every organisation has its own completely private workspace. A driver from one church cannot see passengers from another. Your data belongs to you and only you.

📲

QR code registration

Print your QR code on a church bulletin, a school letter home, or a community noticeboard. Members scan it, enter your invite code, and are registered in two minutes.

🚦

Departure time management

Set a fixed departure time per programme. Drivers see a live countdown and get notified when it is time to leave. Admins can see in real time whether drivers have started on time.

🔔

Live ETA notifications

When the driver is 30, 10, 5 minutes away and arriving, passengers get an instant notification. No more standing outside wondering. No more calls to the driver.

📊

Admin overview dashboard

See every bus, every driver, and every passenger at a glance. Who is going today, who is not, which buses are available, which drivers are online. Assign passengers to drivers with one click. Spot problems before they become missed pickups.

🚌

Driver bus management

Admins assign buses to drivers, or drivers can claim an available unassigned bus themselves. Toggle any bus on or off without removing it from the system. Full fleet visibility from one screen.

♿

Special needs aware

Passengers can add accessibility notes during registration — wheelchair lift needed, specific instructions, behavioural notes. Drivers see these prominently before arriving at each stop.
